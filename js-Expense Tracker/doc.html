Expense Tracker

Welcome to the documentation for our Expense Tracker application! This document provides an overview of the features, functionalities, and usage instructions for our expense tracker built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Features:

  1. Add Expense: To add a new expense, simply click on the "Add Expense" button and fill in the required fields such as expense name, amount, and date.
  2. Edit Expense: To edit an existing expense, select the expense you want to modify from the list, and click on the "Edit" button. You can then make changes to the expense details and save them.
  3. Delete Expense: If you wish to remove an expense from your records, select the expense from the list and click on the "Delete" button. The expense will be permanently removed from the tracker.
  4. Local Storage: Our expense tracker utilizes local storage to store expense data locally on your device. This ensures that your expense records are saved even if you close the application or refresh the page.

Usage Instructions:

  1. Getting Started: To start using the expense tracker, simply open the application in your web browser.
  2. Adding Expenses: Click on the "Add Expense" button and fill in the required details for your expense. Then, click on the "Save" button to add the expense to your tracker.
  3. Editing Expenses: To edit an expense, select the expense from the list and click on the "Edit" button. Update the necessary fields and click on "Save" to apply the changes.
  4. Deleting Expenses: To delete an expense, select the expense from the list and click on the "Delete" button. Confirm the deletion, and the expense will be removed from your tracker.
  5. Saving Data: Your expense data is automatically saved to local storage as you make changes. There's no need to manually save your progress.

We hope this documentation helps you understand and utilize our expense tracker effectively.
